About me

Hello there!

My name is Jasmine Rutherford and I have a very cute dog named Velvet.

One day, at the IMC Kolping dormitory, I decided to make a very exotic meal for lunch. Tofu, with steamed broccoli and rice, what could go wrong... The recipe seemed simple enough, and just because I wasn't the greatest cook didn't mean I couldn't try. So, I grabbed the tofu out of the fridge, and as soon as it hit the cool autumn breeze, it exploded. BOOM!!! Tofu everywhere, on the walls, the ceiling, and all over the floor of the dorm room. It was a disaster! I cried. My plans, my future, all destroyed by a bag of tofu, but the show had to go on. With tofu still all over the dorm room, I started boiling water. The broccoli had to be steamed! Next was the rice and sauce, which I made out of soy sauce, honey, and chopped onion. I was too nervous to try it, so my sister got most of it. It was so good...if only there was tofu to go along with it. My friends (friends no longer) made fun of my tofuless tofu meal. Just because there was only broccoli and rice, and a sad smidge of sauce on my plate, doesn't mean it was bad. After eating, I vowed to never touch tofu again... The end The end
